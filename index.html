About Restaurant Finder

  I created this website and app design for assignments in my SMAD 203 class at James Madison University. I came up with the question "How might we improve the experience of young people wanting to try new restaurants within budget?" When I began trying to answer this question, I thought about my own personal experiences going to new restaurants. I love trying new places to eat, however it can be frustrating. I have often found it hard to find out basic information about restaurants, such as what's on the menu or the price of items. My friends and I are in college on a budget and some with dietary restrictions. Because of this, we usually go to places we are familiar with rather than trying something new. I wanted to create something that would help people like myself explore new restaurants based on their desired elements.
